Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the exam:
  • The exam will consist of multiple-choice questions (MCQs).
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection before starting the exam.
  • Do not refresh the page or click the browser's back button once the exam has started.
  • Switching tabs or opening other applications during the exam is strictly prohibited and may lead to disqualification.
  • The exam is timed. A countdown timer will be visible on the screen. The exam will auto-submit when the time is up.
  • Any malpractice or suspicious activity detected by the system will be recorded and may result in the cancellation of your exam.
  • Make sure you submit the exam manually if you finish before the time expires.
